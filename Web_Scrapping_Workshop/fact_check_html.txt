                        <header>
                            <h1 class="entry-title">The Post of Costa For President is Misleading</h1>

                            

                            <div class="meta-info">

                                <div class="td-post-author-name"><div class="td-author-by">By</div> <a href="https://tsmliberia.com/author/the-stage-media/">Mark Mengonfia</a><div class="td-author-line"> - </div> </div>                                <span class="td-post-date"><time class="entry-date updated td-module-date" datetime="2023-06-30T14:56:10+00:00" >June 30, 2023</time></span>                                                                <div class="td-post-views"><i class="td-icon-views"></i><span class="td-nr-views-4419">8</span></div>                                <div class="td-post-comments"><a href="https://tsmliberia.com/the-post-of-costa-for-president-is-misleading/#respond"><i class="td-icon-comments"></i>0</a></div>                            </div>

                    </div>

            <div class="td-pb-row">

                                <div class="td-ss-main-content">
                                    
        

        <div class="td-post-content td-pb-padding-side">
            <div class="td-post-featured-image"><img width="640" height="360" class="entry-thumb" src="https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=640%2C360&ssl=1" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?w=1200&ssl=1 1200w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=300%2C169&ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=1024%2C576&ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=150%2C84&ssl=1 150w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=768%2C432&ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=747%2C420&ssl=1 747w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=640%2C360&ssl=1 640w, https://i0.wp.com/tsmliberia.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Stage-Media-Liberia-1200-×-628-px-1200-×-675-px-45.png?resize=681%2C383&ssl=1 681w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" alt="" title="The Stage Media-Liberia (1200 × 628 px) (1200 × 675 px) (45)"/></div>
            <div class='mailmunch-forms-before-post' style='display: none !important;'></div>
<p class="has-cyan-bluish-gray-background-color has-background"><strong>Claim:</strong> Henry Costa to contest the 2023 presidential election with his running mate, RiverGee senator Jonathan Sogbie on the All Liberian Party (ALP) ticket.</p>



<p class="has-background" style="background-color:#f60d0d"><strong>Full Text: </strong>Political commentator and talk show host <strong>&nbsp;</strong>Henry P. Costa&#8217;s image appeared on <a href="https://www.facebook.com/100064214280369/posts/pfbid0NANUq21a3E9j9Poo2F5G1FBy2R149pTFKM3ch35nn9R8Uzq7KKsHTj7EwKDKu7Q1l/?d=n" target="_blank" rel="noopener" title="">social media</a> this time, not as a <a href="https://tsmliberia.com/misleading-costa-didnt-reiterate-quest-to-become-vp-to-boakai/">running mate</a> to any political leader but as the presidential candidate of&nbsp; Businessman Benoni Urey&#8217;s party.</p>



<p>Urey established the<a href="https://www.facebook.com/allliberianparty/"> All Liberian Party</a> (ALP) in 2017 when he contested that year&#8217;s presidential election as a presidential candidate.</p>



<p>This claim has made rounds on both <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/CuDQcweMc_x/?igshid=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==">Instagram</a> and <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/1602748173107128/permalink/6202466093135290/">Facebook</a>.</p>



<p>On Instagram, a social media user <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/CuDQcweMc_x/?igshid=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==">posted</a> in pidgin, stating, “So wettin Costa doing his mouth like that for naa? Lay brelor still vex with Boakai oooo.”</p>



<p>This pidgin, when interpreted, means Costa is still angry with former Vice President Joseph Boakai.</p>



<p>Also, <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/1602748173107128/permalink/6202466093135290/">Dream TV </a>posted the same but said they couldn’t verify the claim.</p>



<p><strong>Verification: </strong>Our researcher contacted Leaders of the ALP and Henry P. Costa to verify the claim.</p><div class='mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle' style='display: none !important;'></div>



<p>A WhatsApp message was sent to Costa, who has seen it but has not responded.</p>



<p>If he responds, we will update this article.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image"><img decoding="async" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/GGbw9dQgEs4G4CeCzQ9jRlS0feTEa45u5sF33YzCMzT9G_K5GyaBy2D_TGjMIcn-V2MLL_2AbZxpIy8aIh5Y1NnxYF5HGV-EqMkXgimnO2-2LH6yFwgsoxA9u_Sqe5gwShXb9Xvpg0BaXRZV6XX9SQ" alt=""/></figure>



<p><strong><em>Screenshot of a message sent to Henry Costa with the poster.</em></strong></p>



<p>We also contacted Theodore Momo, ALP chairman who said the claim was not true. “Costa is not the Presidential Candidate of ALP, the information is untrue.</p>



<p>Momo said such a decision could only be made at a national convention rather than a Facebook post.</p>



<p><strong>Conclusion: </strong>Henry Costa and RiverGee senator Jonathan Boy Charles Sogbie are not ALP candidates for president and vice president in the upcoming elections. The claim is Misleading. </p>
